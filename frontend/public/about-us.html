About us

Who are we?

We are two students from Germany who want to change the world of travel with their idea. Since we have traveled a lot ourselves, we know how hard it is sometimes to plan everything perfectly and keep a close eye on the schedule. With our new tool, everyone who likes to travel gets the opportunity to look at great travel destinations even in more exotic areas and book them according to their needs, whether with or without arrival.
Write us personally over our social media channels, which are linked on the contact site, to join our mission to make travelling the world easier for everyone!
